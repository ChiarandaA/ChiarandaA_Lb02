Everything about us and our Production Story

Beginning (2016)

started in a small Workshop in Switzerland, where Diego Chiaranda first tried making Pens, Bowls and similar Objects out of wood. It was very difficult to learn different carving teqchniques. It was very time intensive and took a lot of practice, but slowly the objects where getting better and better.

Practice, practice and more practice (2016-2018)

Over the next few years Diego continued to practice his curving skill and with every time he finished a new object the work was getting easier and better. He started producing pepper and salt mils which where loved from all the people ho got one.

Opening of the new Workshop in Venice (2019/2020)

After over a year of restauration finally the new workshop was ready to be used and work at new creations and products. Now the pens and other Creations where in different Stores around Venice getting selled to the fascinated tourists.

Opening official our own shop (2021)

Some years of selling over friends and different stores and connections passed. Now we are finally selling all our products personally to our costumers. The new online shop built by Aurel Chiaranda makes this easy all over Europe. Now our Costumers dont have to be directly in Venice anymore.

Now

Right now we are expanding our bussines and our products on different ways. So we can reach more future costumers. Stay tuned with us!
